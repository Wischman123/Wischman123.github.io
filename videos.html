The teacher, on camera

Two eras of one classroom, on film.

A preview of the person behind the platform: Brendan Wischweh, first mid-lockdown at a tablet, then three years later explaining to parents what he and a colleague had rebuilt. Neither film loads anything from YouTube until you press play.

2023 — the course, rebuilt before the code was

Years before this platform’s first commit, Brendan and a teaching colleague recorded this for the parents of their students: how, and why, they had significantly changed their Honors Physics course. Different tools, same convictions — the platform on the main page was built to serve the classroom this video describes.

2020 — lockdown walkthroughs, one problem at a time

When COVID emptied the classroom, the teaching moved to video: short walkthroughs, one physics problem each, recorded for students working from home. Here is one from the projectile-motion set. Please excuse the handwriting — it was drawn live on a very old tablet, and the tablet won.
